Research Projects研究项目

Adaptive Hysteresis-Quantized Asymptotic Tracking Control for Non-Strict Feedback Systems via Dynamic Event-Triggering

基于动态事件触发的非严格反馈系统自适应迟滞量化渐近跟踪控制

2025.09 – 2026.06
  • Overview:研究内容： For nonlinear systems with a non-strict feedback structure under bandwidth-limited networks, proposed a four-element collaborative control framework integrating dynamic event-triggering, sector-bounded quantization, barrier Lyapunov functions and RBF-neural-network adaptive approximation — driving the tracking error to asymptotically converge to zero while reducing communication triggers by about 16% compared with conventional relative-threshold event-triggering. 面向带宽受限网络下具有非严格反馈结构的非线性系统，提出一种融合 动态事件触发、扇形量化分解、障碍李雅普诺夫函数与 RBF 神经网络自适应逼近的四要素协同控制框架， 在保证跟踪误差 渐近收敛至零 的同时，相较于传统相对阈值事件触发机制，通信触发次数减少约 16%。
  • My role:个人工作： Independently completed the full controller design and stability proof. Embedded an exponentially-decaying positive variable into the relative-threshold triggering law, and compressed n groups of unknown neural-network weights into two scalar estimates via a dual-aggregation-parameter strategy, significantly reducing computational burden. 独立完成控制器设计与稳定性证明全流程；将指数衰减正变量嵌入相对阈值触发律， 并通过双聚合参数策略将 n 组未知神经网络权值压缩为两个标量估计，显著降低计算负担。
  • Outcome:核心成果： Authored the paper “Adaptive Quantized Event-Triggered Asymptotic Tracking Control for Non-Strict Feedback Nonlinear Systems” as second author (advisor first author), to be submitted to IEEE Transactions on Control of Network Systems. 以第二作者身份（导师一作）撰写论文《Adaptive Quantized Event-Triggered Asymptotic Tracking Control for Non-Strict Feedback Nonlinear Systems》，准备投稿至 IEEE Transactions on Control of Network Systems。

Comparative Study of Machine-Learning Classification Methods on Medical Data

医学数据机器学习分类方法对比研究

2025.03 – 2025.07
  • Overview:研究内容： Targeting the low utilization of healthy-sign data in clinical diagnosis, conducted machine-learning–based prediction of age-related diseases, comparing LightGBM, SVM, MLP and other classifiers — reaching a test-set AUC of 0.98. 针对临床诊断场景中健康体征数据利用率低的问题，开展基于机器学习的年龄相关疾病分类预测研究， 对比 LightGBM、SVM、MLP 等分类算法的性能，最终测试集 AUC 达 0.98。
  • My role:个人工作： As team leader, independently handled the full pipeline — data preprocessing, feature engineering and model building — and designed a threshold-optimization strategy and cross-validation scheme to improve generalization. 作为 项目组长，独立负责数据预处理、特征工程及模型构建全流程开发， 设计阈值优化策略与交叉验证方案以提升模型泛化能力。
